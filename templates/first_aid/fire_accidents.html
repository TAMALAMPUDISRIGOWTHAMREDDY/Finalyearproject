First Aid for Fire Accidents

Steps to Follow:

  1. Stop, Drop, and Roll: If on fire, stop moving, drop to the ground, and roll to extinguish flames.
  2. Remove Burnt Clothing: Do not remove clothing stuck to the skin.
  3. Cool the Burn: Use cool (not ice-cold) water to soothe the burn.
  4. Cover with a Clean Cloth: Use a non-stick, sterile bandage.
  5. Do Not Pop Blisters: This increases infection risk.
  6. Seek Immediate Medical Help: Call emergency services for severe burns.
