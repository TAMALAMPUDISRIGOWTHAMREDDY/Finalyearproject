First Aid for Snake Bites

Steps to Follow:

  1. Stay Calm: Try to remain as still and calm as possible.
  2. Keep the Bite Area Below Heart Level: This helps slow the spread of venom.
  3. Remove Tight Clothing and Jewelry: Swelling can occur, so remove anything that may restrict blood flow.
  4. Do Not Suck the Venom: Avoid cutting the wound or trying to suck out the venom.
  5. Apply a Loose Bandage: Wrap a bandage gently around the bite area, but do not cut off circulation.
  6. Seek Immediate Medical Help: Call emergency services and try to identify the snake if possible.
